Introduction

Bruny Island has on the most preserved Wildlife so on the bucket list would be to feed Tasmanian devils. It has amazing Cliffside views. Boat cruises would take us around the midland and enjoy precious views.

From the national drive, we go head northwest towards the Tasman highway, going through many beautiful roads and enjoying the view. We then exit onto the Arthur Highway reaching Port Arthur within an hour.

Local Attractions

Mount Wellington: Stroll through cool forested gullies along historic walking tracks or traverse Wellington Range in the saddle on horse or mountain bike. Four wheel drive along rough mountain trails, climb leaning dolerite towers, or sit quietly among the birds. Find the perfect place for a picnic or to flip a burger.

Salamanca Markets: Tasmania’s own market is a selection of your favorite Salamanca Market stallholders and is running each Saturday from 8.30 am to 3 pm. Our new look market consists of over 220 stallholders representing artisans, designers and producers - it’s as Tasmanian as we can make it.
